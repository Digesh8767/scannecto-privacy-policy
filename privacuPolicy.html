1. What Information Do We Collect?

Personal Information You Provide

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register for an account
  • Use our scanning services
  • Contact us for support
  • Subscribe to premium features

Types of Information We Collect:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, password
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method (processed by app stores)
  • Usage Data: Device type, app version, feature usage
  • Scanned Content: Documents, QR codes, and barcodes (stored locally or in your Google Drive)

Important: We do not store your scanned documents on our servers. All scanned content remains under your control and is stored locally on your device or in your connected Google Drive account.

Google API Services

Our use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

2. How Do We Process Your Information?

We process your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: Create and maintain your account, authenticate users
  • Service Delivery: Provide scanning functionality and app features
  • Communication: Send important updates, support responses
  • Advertising: Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Security: Protect against fraud and ensure service security
  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal obligations and protect rights

3. When and With Whom Do We Share Your Information?

We may share your information in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: Google (AdMob, Google Drive, authentication), app store platforms
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly choose to share information

We never sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app may include third-party content, advertisements, or links (e.g., Google AdMob, Google Drive). We are not responsible for the content, accuracy, or policies of these third parties. Please review their privacy policies before providing any information.

5. Social Media Logins

If you choose to register using social media accounts (Google, Facebook, etc.), we may receive certain profile information from the social media provider. We use this information only as described in this Privacy Policy. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of your social media providers.

6. How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. We will delete or anonymize your information when it's no longer needed.

7. Information from Minors

We do not knowingly collect data from or market to children under 18 years of age. By using our Services, you represent that you are at least 18 or have parental consent. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will delete such information promptly.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information provided in the Contact Us section.

9. Do-Not-Track Features

We do not currently respond to Do-Not-Track browser signals. If a standard for online tracking is adopted that we must follow, we will inform you in a revised version of this Privacy Policy.

10. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Your scanned content is stored locally on your device or in your Google Drive account, not on our servers.

11. Children's Privacy

Scannecto is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under this age. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and, if required by law, by other means. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
